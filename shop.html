FAQs about orders and shipping

  • How can I pay?

    You can either pay with Paypal or by Credit/Debit e-Transfer.

  • Do you offer shipping?

    Yes, the shipping fee is $3.00 CAD for all orders within Canada (no shipping outside of Canada at the moment). We also offer arrangements for pick up at designated locations, for no charge. To finalize order logistics, we will email you and send you an Instagram DM.

  • Can I refund or exchange my order?

    No, all items are on final sale - we do not offer returns or exchange. However, under extenuating circumstances, we may make exceptions.

  • What if I want to change my order?

    You can change your order up until we process the payment.

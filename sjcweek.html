What is Social Justice Week?

Social Justice week is a 7-day event where we encourage students to take part in various activities related to Social Justice issues. On Monday, we will be educating students on issues currently faced by Canadaâ€™s First Nations, Metis, and Indigenous peoples by organizing a virtual documentary screening using TeleParty and Discord, in order for students to understand what life is like for these people.
